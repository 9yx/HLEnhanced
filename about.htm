About

Giving Half-Life 1 the love it deserves.

Half-Life: Enhanced is a project that give more power to Half-Life 1 modders for their creations and bring a lot of fixes and improvements for the players as well.
The main features are cleaning Valve's code, provide better documentation (powered by Doxygen), fix bugs and crashes and much more that you can check in the "Features" page of the wiki.
Thanks to the open source model, anyone regardless of Half-Life 1 modding experience can contribute to the project.
Half-Life: Enhanced is a project created by Solokiller with contributions from Half-Life 1 fans from all over the world.
